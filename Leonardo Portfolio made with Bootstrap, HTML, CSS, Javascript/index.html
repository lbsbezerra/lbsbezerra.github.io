Resilient

I have no problems working with environments where pressure or tight timelines are involved. I'm really good handling multiple tasks at the same time; and as my wife likes to say... "You are such a patient guy!", so I can help these clients that are stressed with something (specially when they try to "vent" to the Support Engineer team, if you know what mean!).

Self-Motivated

For new technologies and opportunities to deliver solutions to the clients! I love to serve and to learn! I always worked as an Operations guy, and development is still something new for me. But I.T. is my passion, so learning something new such as HTML, CSS and JavaScript to create this portfolio (and also a little bit of Bootstrap) was an interesting challenge for me!

Strategic Thinking

For troubleshooting problems! Have I told you that in another company I worked for in the past, I created an interesting Knowledge Base (Internal for our colleagues, and External for our clients) that helped us to deliver value in our services? I believe that if an issue has been solved before, there is no need to stress yourself out so much trying to figure out how to solve it again: Just remember to use the Knowledge Base and to troubleshoot properly!
